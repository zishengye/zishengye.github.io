About me

Hi I am Zisheng Ye, a fourth year PhD student at Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Wisconsin-Madison. Now, I am working with Prof. Wenxiao Pan on modeling and optimization of biomimetic nanomaterials. Prior to join Pan's Lab, I was a master student work in the Soft Machines Lab at Carnegie Mellon Univesrity under the supervision of Prof. Carmel Majidi on designing and optimization soft robots. I got my bachelor degree in Engineering Mechanics from Beijing Institute of Technology, China. There, I helped the design and implementation of a parallel programme to do the simulation of large-scale flexible multi-body dynamic systems.

Research interest

Computational Mechanics, Quantum Computing, Robotics
