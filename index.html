About me

Hi I am Zisheng Ye(叶子晟), a fourth year PhD candidate at Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Wisconsin-Madison. Now, I am working with Prof. Wenxiao Pan on high performance computing, especially in large-scale fluid-solid interactions and quantum computing. My current focus is to bridge data and physics at scale. Prior to join Pan's Lab, I was a master student work in the Soft Machines Lab at Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Prof. Carmel Majidi on designing and optimization soft robots. I got my bachelor degree in Engineering Mechanics from Beijing Institute of Technology, China. There, I helped the design and implementation of a parallel programme to do the simulation of large-scale flexible multi-body dynamic systems.

Research interest

Computational Mechanics, Quantum Computing, Robotics
